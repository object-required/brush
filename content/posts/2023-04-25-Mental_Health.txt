We don't feel too good.

Back in the day, we gave up on our aspirations and ourselves
as a person. Now, seeing other people's long-term goals, or

are no feelings, except for bitter sadness and viscous
desperation somewhere on the edge of the consciousness.

It's okay, though: at least no one is willing to harm themselves
or our body; the latter we used to do a lot.

to replace with what...
