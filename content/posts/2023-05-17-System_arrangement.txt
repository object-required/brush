Having spent some time away from social networks gave us the
opportunity to inspect how we work as a system. It appears we
all reside in the userspace, i.e., conscious and preconscious.

+-----------+--------------+--------------+
| Conscious | Preconscious | Subconscious |
+-----------+--------------+--------------+
| * Nik                    |              |
| * Ena                    |              |
|     * Fox                |              |
|     * Sage               |              |
|         * Vixen          |              |
+--------------------------+--------------+

While Vixen has never fronted yet, we observe the rest of us
function differently on a scale between human-like and full
animal modes.

In all cases, though, we have to interface with a single body
that we share. Also, the environment we spend most time in does
not account for us as individuals. We suppose that's a struggle
way too familiar for many systems, and everyone has their unique
approach or solution to this.

We decided to try arranging ourselves in an order that would
make more sense to the biological (animal, haha) part of us,
and social interactions IRL.

+-------+-------+
| Nik   | Ena   |  Daily activities, goals, socializing, etc.
+-------+-------+
| Sage  | Vixen |  Consciense, wisdom, support
+-------+-------+
| Fox           |  Bodily care: needs, desires, etc.
+---------------+

This arrangement should allow us to specialize in parts of life
we are most interested in and capable of. Hopefully, we'll be
more functional and have fun in the process. After all, working
together is nice, isn't it?

While it all may sound mechanistic, it does resonate with every
headmate well. Fox is horny, for instance - that's always a good
sign for it.

~Horny Fox
~Enthusiastic Ena
~Wondering Nik
~Proud Sage
~Joyful Vixen
